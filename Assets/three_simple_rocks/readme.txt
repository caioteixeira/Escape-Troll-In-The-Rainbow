Three simple Rocks package
--------------------------

Included:

- 3 different rock models
- 3 diffuse 1024*1024 texture (with 512*512 version too)
- 3 normalmap 1024*1024 texture
- demo example

Instructions :

Just 3 rock models with their own diffuse and normalmap textures.
You can use the 512*512 diffuse textures included in the package.
